GAME DEVELOPMENT

Veil Runner | Global Game Jam

Built during the Global Game Jam 2026 — a worldwide 48-hour game development event. A post-apocalyptic 2D platformer shooter built in Python (Pygame), shipped from scratch under pressure.

A radiation-ravaged dockyard city. A mask fused to your face. Everyone else is dead or mutated. You're not trying to save the city — just escape it. Fight through two levels of zombies, mutants, and bosses, scavenging weapons and upgrading your mask along the way. The mask keeps you alive. It also never comes off.

  • 2 levels — The Outskirts and The City — each with a unique boss encounter
  • Boss fights: Glitch Merchant (teleporting, corruption bullets) and Mech Titan (2,000 HP, plasma + flamethrower)
  • Random upgrade system — collect hearts to unlock speed, damage, or shield boosts
  • Combo kill-streak system that scales score and damage output
  • Full keyboard and controller support with haptic rumble feedback
  • Runs at 90 FPS with animated GIF sprites — built with PyCharm, Pygame, and Piskel
